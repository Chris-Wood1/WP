Muscle Cars

I dont believe you will find a definition for "muscle car" in any dictionary. There are probably many descriptions of a muscle car. This list is some of the important characteristics that most "muscle cars" share.

  • A V8 engine under the hood (the bigger the better)
  • A lighter car (better weight-to-horsepower ratio)
  • The car was manufactured starting in 1962-1964 and ending in about 1974
  • Rear wheel drive
  • A gear ratio about 3:50 to 4.11
These vehicles had either an automatic or manual transmission. The manual was prefered for lighting up those rear tires. It was always better having a posi-traction rear axle.
